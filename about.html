About Mojo

Mojo is a simple way to recognise people who make things better — quietly, kindly, and without fuss.

Whether it’s a waiter who remembered your name, a nurse who stayed a little longer, or a mechanic who explained things clearly — Mojo helps you say thanks in a way that gets noticed, not lost.

It’s not about ratings or reviews. It’s about moments, gratitude, and giving people a boost.

Mojo is designed for hospitality, healthcare, retail — anywhere people make a difference. Just scan a tag, leave a kind word, and we’ll pass it on.

Built and hosted in Australia, Mojo was created with a single goal: to make sharing appreciation easy — and feel good doing it.

If you'd like to use Mojo at your venue, or just want to know more, get in touch.
