The Mojo System

Mojo is a trust signal. A reputation marker. A quiet way to say: "This person works well."

From tradies to teachers, cleaners to carers — the Mojo system lets anyone carry a simple mark of trust. No reviews, no ratings. Just a clear, respectful signal: I do my job, and I do it well.
