How Mojo Works

Mojo is a simple, human way to give and receive feedback — anytime, anywhere.

For Customers

Each worker or business displays a Mojo ID, usually in the form of a sticker or card. If you’ve had a great experience, just scan it. You’ll be taken to a page where you can leave a message of thanks or encouragement.

Your message goes directly to the person or business. It’s private by default, and designed to make someone’s day — not to gather data or show off online.

For Workers

Mojo helps you build a portable, positive record of the pride you take in your work. Each Mojo ID is yours — and it stays with you even if you change jobs or locations.

You can show your Mojo to future employers, colleagues, or customers. It’s like a quiet reputation tool that travels with you.

How It's Different

  • No ratings, no reviews, no public scores.
  • No one sees your feedback unless you want them to.
  • No app needed — just scan and send.
  • Built to encourage gratitude, not complaints.
  • Respects your privacy. We don’t track or sell anything.

For Everyone Else

Whether you’re a parent, teacher, nurse, baker, driver, cleaner or CEO — Mojo is for you. If someone makes your day a little better, let them know. If someone helps, say thanks. That’s it.

Global & Simple

Mojo IDs work anywhere. You don’t need a login, and you don’t need to understand the tech. Just use it once — you’ll get it forever.
