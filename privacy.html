Privacy Policy

Mojo.Spot is built on trust. We collect as little as possible, and we never use your data for marketing or tracking. Here’s exactly how we handle your info.

Who We Are

Mojo.Spot is a simple system for sharing appreciation and recognising good work. We are based in Australia and committed to ethical, respectful data practices.

What We Collect — and Why

  • Email address: To connect you to your Mojo ID and deliver messages
  • Preferred name: To personalise your experience (e.g. “Hi Sam!”)
  • Messages you submit: To pass your feedback on to the intended recipient
  • Mojo ID: To associate feedback with a specific worker
  • Date & time: For basic logging and message retrieval

What We Don’t Do

  • We don’t sell your data
  • We don’t track you across the web
  • We don’t collect unnecessary sensitive information
  • We don’t send marketing emails unless you explicitly ask for them

How We Protect Your Info

  • All data is encrypted and stored securely
  • We use trusted tools like Supabase and Vercel
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time

Finder & Message Retention

If you're someone scanning a tag and leaving a message, your info is stored briefly then erased:

  • Finder contact details are automatically deleted after 7 days
  • Messages for workers are kept for them to read privately

Our Promise

We’re not here to follow you. We’re here to help you lift someone up — and walk away smiling.
